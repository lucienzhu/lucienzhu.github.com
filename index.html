Bio

I am working in AI Platform Department in Tencent TEG Group and work with AI Lab. My reseach interest is to develop HPC system for deep learning research and production.

I used to be a Ph.D student supervised by Dr. Kenneth Wong in Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong. I worked on hand detection and localization for color cameras. Before this, most of my work was to recognize hand postures based on a depth camera. More details are in my research page.
