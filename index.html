Bio

I made AI for serveral games, and I am working on something new.

I served as Vice President for Parametrix.ai (Round B) exploring AGI, especially in gaming industry. We have been developing Bots, NPCs, Companions and Smart Cities for the gamers in various genres, e.g., MOBA, FPS, SLG, Card Games, etc. It was a lot of fun to explore agent and world modeling in virtual game environment.

I used to work at Tencent AI Platform Department as Technical Lead of vision engineering team and cooperating with vision researchers at Tencent AI Lab. I helped to develop Tencent Fine Art, a super-human AI for the game of Go. In 2018, I also served as a TAC member of LF Deep Learning Foundation. Before joining Tencent, I was a Ph.D student supervised by Dr. Kenneth Wong in Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong. I worked on hand detection and localization for color cameras. Before this, most of my work was to recognize hand postures based on a depth camera. More details are in my research page.
