Bio

I am a Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong. Dr. Kenneth Wong is my current supervisor. My reseach interest is to bring practical machine methods to computer vision research.

I am working on hand detection and localization for color cameras. Before this, most of my work was to recognize hand postures based on a depth camera. More details are in my research page.
